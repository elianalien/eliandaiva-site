Elian Daiva is an interaction designer and creative technologist based on Berlin, Germany. He holds MSc. and BSc. in Electrical Engineering from Institut Teknologi Bandung, Indonesia. His works span from interactive installations, embedded systems, games, and virtual reality.

With his fellow, Elian works through design thinking and human-centered design to explore the structures and systems of human interactions and the potential for technology to mediate, manipulate, and evolve these interactions.

Elian has worked on installation at London Design Biennale 2016, Bandung International Art Festival 2016, Venice Art Biennale 2017, and Bandung Planning Gallery 2018.
